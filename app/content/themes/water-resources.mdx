  ## About Water Availability
  NASA Water Insight provides data from satellites and models on the availability of freshwater, in near real time, for human uses in the U.S. and globally. This information is particularly important in areas where water is not otherwise monitored regularly. Water managers need estimates of surface water, groundwater, precipitation, evapotranspiration, and runoff to allocate resources and understand changes that may affect resource allocation. NASA mission data used in these estimates include GRACE, GRACE-FO, ….. Key stakeholders and partners in co-developing water resources data products and applications are the U.S. Bureau of Reclamation, U.S. Geological Survey, Army Corps of Engineers, state water resources and drinking water managers, municipal water suppliers, and industrial users of water.
