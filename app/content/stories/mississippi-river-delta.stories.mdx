    ### Delta under Pressure 
    <br />

    NASA Earth observations and models show increased flooded area over the Delta. Land loss and flooding have direct impacts on people, their livelihoods, as well as on the Delta ecosystem and natural resources.

    ### Scenario Modeling
    #### Modeling Framework
    To analyze the impacts from hydrology, sea level rise, and water management, we modeled four what-if scenarios using the LIS framework and the Hydrological Modeling and Analysis Platform (HyMAP) routing model.
    </Prose>

        <Caption>
            Among the factors that impact the Delta, we only consider hydrology, sea level rise, and water management in the scenario modeling framework.

    ##### Impact of Water Management
